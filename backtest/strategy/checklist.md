# Pre-Trade Checklist — Breakout Momentum

Mark each item DONE (☑) or N/A (⊠) before placing a trade.
Every ☑ is mandatory unless marked ⊠.

---

## □ MARKET REGIME

- [ ] SPY is above its 50-day SMA (bull regime confirmed)
- [ ] VIX < 28 (no panic filter — or ⊠ if data unavailable)
- [ ] No major market holiday / early close scheduled

---

## □ INDEX CONFIRMATION

- [ ] QQQ is above its 20-day moving average
- [ ] IWM (small-cap) not in sharp downtrend vs SPY
- [ ] No major gap-down on SPY/QQQ at open (> −2 %)

---

## □ EARNINGS FILTER

- [ ] Trade date is NOT within ±5 calendar days of any held ticker's earnings date
- [ ] No unexpected earnings announcement scheduled for today

---

## □ TICKER-SPECIFIC CHECKS (per candidate)

### Entry Condition Checklist

- [ ] Close > 20-day high (confirmed breakout)
- [ ] Volume > 1.5× 20-day volume SMA
- [ ] Stock 20-day return > QQQ 20-day return (relative strength)
- [ ] Today's gain ≤ +8 % (not chasing)
- [ ] ATR-based stop distance > 0
- [ ] Reward-to-risk ≥ 2.0× (pre-check, not mandatory)

### Fundamental (optional, v1 skip)

- [ ] No news/catalyst that explains the breakout (earnings, FDA, etc.)
- [ ] No recent stock split or dividend adjustment pending

---

## □ PORTFOLIO RULES

- [ ] Current open positions < 5
- [ ] This trade's position size ≤ 5 % of equity
- [ ] Risk per trade ≤ 0.5 % of equity
- [ ] Not adding to a losing position

---

## □ TRADE EXECUTION

- [ ] Enter at close price (market-on-close equivalent)
- [ ] Stop price pre-set: max(close − 1.5×ATR, low_20 × 0.98)
- [ ] Target price pre-set: entry + 2.5 × (entry − stop)
- [ ] Target and stop both recorded in journal before order fill
- [ ] No counter-trend signals on the 15m / 60m chart (v1 skip)

---

## □ JOURNAL

- [ ] Ticker, entry date, entry price, stop, target, ATR recorded
- [ ] Max position size calculated and recorded
- [ ] Trade setup rationale briefly noted (1 sentence)
- [ ] Assigned trade ID (sequential integer)
